OUR MISSION

Bridging the Communication Divide

Technology is only as powerful as the number of people it can empower. My journey began with a simple realization: millions of individuals in the deaf and hard-of-hearing communities are systematically excluded from daily interactions due to communication barriers.

I built Signova to tear down these walls. By combining advanced AI, machine learning, and an unwavering commitment to inclusive design, we are transforming isolated silence into seamless, real-time dialogue.

I believe true innovation happens at the intersection of empathy and engineering. My mission is to build technology that doesn't just process data, but actively restores human connection.
